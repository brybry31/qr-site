Disaster Preparedness

Scan this QR code to access typhoon safety guides, evacuation routes, and emergency hotlines specific to Makati barangays. Quick access ensures that students, residents, and officials in the Mapua University Makati area can respond faster and reduce confusion during emergencies.

Community Announcements

Local governments and barangay offices in Makati can update QR‑linked sites with real‑time advisories such as class suspensions at Mapua University, relief distribution schedules at barangay halls, and evacuation updates for communities near the Pasig River.

Impact on Society

QR systems empower citizens in Metro Manila to act quickly and responsibly when disasters strike. By strengthening community response in areas like Makati, they help reduce panic and improve coordination between schools, barangays, and city offices.

Nation-Building Role

Integrating QR technology into disaster preparedness builds resilience against natural hazards in the Philippines. In Makati, this approach protects vulnerable populations, promotes a culture of safety, and demonstrates how universities like Mapua can contribute to nation‑building through innovation.
