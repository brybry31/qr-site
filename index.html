QR Code System

Innovation and Application in the Philippines

Disaster Preparedness

Scan this QR code to access typhoon safety guides, evacuation routes, and emergency hotlines. Quick access saves lives and reduces confusion during disasters.

Community Announcements

Local governments can update QR-linked sites with real-time advisories such as class suspensions, relief distribution schedules, and evacuation updates.

Impact on Society

QR systems empower citizens to act quickly and responsibly when disasters strike, strengthening community response and resilience.

Nation-Building Role

Integrating QR technology into disaster preparedness builds resilience against natural hazards, protects vulnerable populations, and promotes a culture of safety.
