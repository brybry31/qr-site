Health Tips

Boil water before drinking to prevent waterborne diseases, especially during flooding in Makati barangays.

Keep wounds clean and covered to avoid infection in crowded evacuation centers.

Wear masks in evacuation sites such as Makati High School Gym to reduce the spread of illness.

Follow advisories from the Makati Health Department and barangay health workers for updated safety measures.
