Preparedness Materials

Downloadable infographics and guides on typhoon safety, evacuation drills, and community resilience are tailored for Mapua University Makati students and nearby barangays. Materials include evacuation drill schedules from Makati City Hall, barangay preparedness manuals, and student‑friendly safety posters used in Mapua coursework.

Interactive Learning

Future integration includes interactive quizzes and videos designed for Mapua University classrooms and Makati communities. These resources aim to teach disaster response skills, simulate evacuation scenarios, and strengthen collaboration between students, barangay officials, and local residents.
